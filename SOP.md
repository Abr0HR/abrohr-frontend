# AbrO HR - Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

## System Overview

AbrO HR is a professional employee attendance tracking system designed for both employers and employees. The system features:

- Multi-mode punch (Office, WFH, On-Duty)
- Shift management with flexible scheduling
- Work-from-home flexibility
- Indian Labour Law compliance
- Real-time attendance analytics
- Employee profile management

**Website:** https://www.abrohr.com

---

## PART 1: EMPLOYER/ADMIN USER GUIDE

### Getting Started as an Employer

#### 1. Accessing the Dashboard
1. Visit https://www.abrohr.com
2. Click on "Dashboard" tab
3. You will see the attendance overview dashboard

#### 2. Dashboard Metrics

The employer dashboard displays:

- **Present Today:** Shows count of employees marked present
- **Absent Today:** Shows count of absent employees
- **On Leave:** Shows count of employees on approved leave
- **Total Staff:** Shows total active employees in your organization
- **Percentage changes:** Compared to previous day

#### 3. Quick Actions

Available quick actions:
- **Clock In:** Manual entry for office entry
- **Clock Out:** Manual entry for office exit
- **Settings:** Configure system preferences
- **Reports:** Generate attendance reports

### Navigation Tabs

#### A. Dashboard Tab
- **Purpose:** View daily attendance summary
- **Data Shown:** Present, Absent, On Leave, Total Staff counts
- **Use:** Quickly check attendance status

#### B. Analytics Tab
- **Purpose:** Analyze attendance trends and patterns
- **Features:** 
  - Weekly/Monthly attendance trends
  - Attendance rates by department
  - Late arrivals analysis
  - Absenteeism trends

#### C. Employees Tab
- **Purpose:** Manage employee profiles and records
- **Features:**
  - Add new employees
  - Edit employee details
  - View employee attendance history
  - Manage shift assignments
  - Update work mode preferences (Office/WFH/On-Duty)

#### D. Settings Tab
- **Purpose:** Configure system settings
- **Features:**
  - Organization settings
  - Shift configuration
  - Holiday management
  - Leave policies
  - Notification preferences

### Managing Employees

#### Adding a New Employee

1. Go to **Employees** tab
2. Click **Add New Employee** button
3. Fill in employee details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Employee ID
   - Department
   - Designation
   - Date of Joining
   - Shift Assignment
   - Work Mode Preference (Office/WFH)
4. Click **Save**

#### Viewing Employee Records

1. Go to **Employees** tab
2. Select employee from list
3. View:
   - Personal information
   - Attendance records
   - Monthly attendance summary
   - Leave balance
   - Shift history

#### Editing Employee Information

1. Go to **Employees** tab
2. Find employee and click **Edit**
3. Modify required fields
4. Click **Update**

### Attendance Management

#### Recording Attendance

**Automated Punch:** Employees can clock in/out themselves

**Manual Entry:**
1. Go to Employees tab
2. Select employee
3. Click **Manual Entry**
4. Select date and punch mode:
   - **Office:** On-site attendance
   - **WFH:** Work from home
   - **On-Duty:** Official duties outside office
5. Enter time details
6. Click **Save**

### Understanding Punch Modes

- **Office:** Employee present at office location
- **WFH (Work From Home):** Employee working from home (trust-based, no monitoring)
- **On-Duty:** Employee on authorized official duties

### Generating Reports

#### Monthly Attendance Report
1. Go to **Reports** section
2. Select **Attendance Report**
3. Choose month and year
4. Options:
   - View on screen
   - Download as PDF
   - Download as Excel
   - Print

#### Attendance Analysis
1. Go to **Analytics**
2. View pre-built dashboards:
   - Attendance trends
   - Department-wise attendance
   - Employee-wise attendance
   - Late arrivals
   - Absenteeism patterns

### Leave Management

#### Approving Leave Requests
1. Go to **Employees** > **Leave Requests**
2. View pending leave requests
3. Review:
   - Leave type
   - Duration
   - Reason
4. Click **Approve** or **Reject**
5. Add remarks if needed
6. System sends notification to employee

#### Setting Leave Policies
1. Go to **Settings** > **Leave Policies**
2. Configure:
   - Annual leave balance
   - Sick leave balance
   - Casual leave balance
   - Leave accrual rules
3. Save policy

### Shift Management

#### Creating a Shift
1. Go to **Settings** > **Shift Configuration**
2. Click **Add New Shift**
3. Enter:
   - Shift Name (e.g., Morning, Evening, Night)
   - Start Time
   - End Time
   - Break Duration
   - Days of operation
4. Click **Save**

#### Assigning Shifts to Employees
1. Go to **Employees**
2. Select employee
3. Click **Edit**
4. Select **Shift Assignment**
5. Choose shift from dropdown
6. Set effective date
7. Click **Save**

### Labour Law Compliance

The system automatically ensures:

- **48-hour Work Week:** Tracks to ensure no employee exceeds 48 working hours per week
- **Overtime Calculation:** Automatically calculates and marks overtime at 2x rate
- **Night Shift Restrictions:** Monitors night shift assignments according to law
- **Break Compliance:** Ensures mandatory breaks are recorded

## PART 2: EMPLOYEE USER GUIDE

### Getting Started as an Employee

#### 1. Accessing the System
1. Visit https://www.abrohr.com
2. Click "Employees" tab (if you're an employee)
3. Or use your personalized employee portal link

#### 2. Employee Dashboard

Your personal dashboard shows:
- Your today's punch status
- Remaining leave balance
- Upcoming shifts
- Personal attendance summary
- Leave applications status

### Daily Attendance Process

#### Clocking In (Start of Day)

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Click **Clock In** button
3. Select punch mode:
   - **Office:** You're at office
   - **WFH:** Working from home
   - **On-Duty:** On official duties
4. System records current time
5. Confirmation message appears

#### Clocking Out (End of Day)

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Click **Clock Out** button
3. System records current time
4. Attendance marked for the day

### Working From Home (WFH)

#### Requesting WFH

1. Go to **Settings** > **Work Preferences**
2. Click **Request WFH**
3. Select date(s)
4. Add reason (optional)
5. Click **Submit**
6. Wait for manager approval (if required)
7. Once approved, you can clock in as WFH

#### WFH Punch Process

1. On WFH approved day, click **Clock In**
2. Select **WFH** mode
3. Clock in as usual
4. Work as normal
5. Clock out when day ends
6. Same as office attendance

### Leave Management

#### Checking Leave Balance

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Leave Balance**
2. View available leaves:
   - Annual Leave
   - Sick Leave
   - Casual Leave
   - Special Leave (if any)

#### Applying for Leave

1. Go to **Leave** > **New Leave Application**
2. Fill details:
   - Select leave type
   - Choose dates (from-to)
   - Add reason
   - Attach documents if required
3. Click **Submit**
4. Check status: Pending/Approved/Rejected
5. Notification will be sent

#### Leave Application Status

- **Pending:** Awaiting manager review
- **Approved:** Leave granted, don't need to come to office
- **Rejected:** Reapply with valid reason
- **Cancelled:** You cancelled the request

### Viewing Your Attendance

#### Monthly Attendance Summary

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Attendance Summary**
2. Select month
3. View:
   - Days present
   - Days absent
   - Days on leave
   - Total working hours
   - Overtime (if any)

#### Detailed Attendance Records

1. Go to **Attendance Records**
2. Select date range
3. View for each day:
   - Clock in time
   - Clock out time
   - Total hours worked
   - Punch mode (Office/WFH/On-Duty)

### On-Duty Management

#### Requesting On-Duty

1. Go to **Leave** > **On-Duty Request**
2. Fill:
   - Date(s)
   - Purpose/Location
   - Expected return time
3. Click **Submit**
4. Wait for approval
5. Once approved, clock in as "On-Duty"

### Personal Settings

#### Update Profile

1. Go to **Settings** > **Profile**
2. View/Edit:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Address
   - Emergency contact
3. Click **Save**

#### Change Password

1. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter:
   - Current password
   - New password
   - Confirm new password
4. Click **Update**

#### Notification Preferences

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Choose which notifications to receive:
   - Punch reminders
   - Leave approval notifications
   - Attendance alerts
3. Choose delivery method:
   - Email
   - SMS
   - In-app
4. Click **Save**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Issues
- **Can't remember password:** Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- **Account locked:** Contact HR administrator
- **Can't log in:** Ensure correct email and password

#### Attendance Issues
- **Clock in not working:** Check internet connection, refresh page
- **Punch mode not selectable:** Contact administrator
- **Attendance not reflecting:** Check after 5 minutes, may be syncing

#### Leave Issues
- **Can't apply leave:** Ensure leave balance available
- **Leave not showing in balance:** Wait for manager approval
- **Need to cancel leave:** Go to Leave > Pending Applications > Cancel

### Contacting Support

- **Email:** support@abrohr.com
- **Phone:** Your company HR department
- **In-app:** Click Help button on any page

## Important Information

### Data Privacy
- All attendance data is stored securely
- Only authorized personnel can view your information
- Your WFH mode data is trust-based with minimal monitoring
- Compliant with DPDP (Data Privacy & Protection) Act

### System Access
- Accessible 24/7 from any device
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Works on all modern browsers
- No special software required

### Best Practices

For Employers:
- Regularly monitor attendance dashboards
- Address absenteeism promptly
- Ensure all employees are trained
- Maintain proper shift assignments
- Review analytics for trends

For Employees:
- Clock in/out promptly
- Update personal information
- Apply for leaves in advance
- Check leave balance before applying
- Report attendance issues immediately

## Glossary

- **On-Duty:** Official duties outside office premises
- **Leave Balance:** Remaining leaves available
- **Shift:** Fixed working hours assignment
- **Overtime:** Hours worked beyond standard shift
- **DPDP:** Data Privacy & Protection Act (India)
